Privacy Policy

FOMSY Bank takes your online privacy very seriously. Our aim is to provide a safe and secure environment for all our visitors and members.

This Privacy Policy is intended to outline what kind of information we may gather about you when you visit our site, and what we do with it.

When you enter data into any form on FOMSY Bank, we receive and store it. This information is used to create and maintain your member profile and remains confidential at all times

Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Typically they are used to remember some specific information relating to you and your use of a site, or to record your username and/or password. On our site we only use cookies to record your username whilst you are logged in. This is solely so that you only have to log in once per session. We do not use Cookies to track any personal data.

Spam

Spam refers to the practice of sending unsolicited email to a broad range of email addresses. Typically this is practiced by unscrupulous advertisers, wishing to promote their products and/or services. FOMSY Bank operates a no-spam policy and will never disclose either your email address or member information, unless required to do so by law. The only communications that you will receive from the site will be those that you specifically elect to receive; either by being notified of updates that match your preferences or some other communication that you have initiated.

Phishing

Phishing is a type of fraud with the goal of obtaining personal information, credentials, credit card number, or financial data. The attackers lure, or fish, for sensitive data through various different methods. Phishers create convincing e-mails or SMS requesting potential victims to click a link to update their bank account information. Victims click these links and are presented with a form requesting bank account numbers, Credit Card numbers, credentials, and other types of data that can be used in identity theft crimes. Phishers, also, create web sites that look very similar to legitimate sites and lure victims to them through e-mail messages and other web sites to gain the same type of information.

FOMSY Bank does not collect private client information or confidential user data via emails or SMS. FOMSY Bank does not send any URL in emails or SMS to clients. Also, FOMSY Bank website does not require any user credentials. Accordingly, DO NOT open any link requesting such information in the email which- if clicked -sends the client to a fake website that request confidential information. Do NOT respond to these emails or SMS. They should be simply deleted.
